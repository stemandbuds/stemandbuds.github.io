About Us

STEM and Buds is a one-on-one science mentoring program for elementary and middle school students that will allow each and every individual to create and execute their own unique project on a STEM-related topic. Designed to redefine the current classroom setting, the program focuses on personal enrichment and mutual collaboration between students.

Starting a Chapter

Our organization will assist in providing the necessary materials for students to create their projects. Students should not be deterred due to a lack of resources. We will also help to organize a mock science fair at the end of the program for students to show off their projects.

After-School Schedule

Our program will consist of nine 1.5 hour sessions. The first 3 sessions will be dedicated to exposing the students to various STEM fields. In the following 3 sessions, students will brainstorm, select an idea, develop a simple research plan, and prepare to perform an experiment. The next 2 sessions will be for students to perform their experiment and record results. The final session will be used for students to create a poster to present their findings. This poster would be optionally displayed to parents and other participants in a mock science fair.

Summer Camp

Our program will also have a summer camp consisting of five 6 hour sessions that provide a more comprehensive learning experience. Students will be able to engage in more in-depth opportunities in addition to creating a more full-fledged project.

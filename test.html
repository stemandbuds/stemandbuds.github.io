About Us

STEM and Buds is a one-on-one science mentoring program for elementary and middle school students in efforts to allow each and every individual to create and execute their own project on a STEM related topic. Designed to redefine the current classroom setting, the program focuses on personal enrichment and mutual collaboration between students.
